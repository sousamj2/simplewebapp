Server Rules

By playing on Mostly Jovial Crafters, you agree to follow these rules. Violations may result in a ban.

1

Mutual Respect

Be friendly and respect all players. Harassment, racism, sexism, bullying, or any form of discrimination is strictly prohibited and should be reported to staff immediately. Violations will lead to a permanent ban.

2

No Griefing or Stealing

The land-claiming system should prevent most incidents, but please do not destroy other players' builds or steal items from their chests — even if those chests are unprotected. Always respect others' work.

3

Mods & Hacks

The use of modified clients, hacks, x-ray textures, or any unfair advantage is prohibited.

Allowed mods:

  • Performance mods (Optifine, Sodium, etc.)
  • Minimaps (without entity radar / cave mapping)
  • Freecam (building aid only — please configure it to not allow passing through walls)
4

Bug Exploitation

Do not abuse game or server bugs for personal gain — this includes item duplication, seed cracking, or similar exploits.

Exception: TNT duplication is allowed within limits (e.g. only on your own claimed land).

5

PvP (Player vs Player)

Combat between players is only allowed if both parties agree. Use /pvp toggle to opt in. By default, all new players have a 15-minute protection timer after joining.

Twice a week, a server event will enable PvP for everyone — you will only be safe in spawn or on claimed land. More details on the Server Guide page.

6

Player Inactivity (AFK)

This is an active player server. Players who are AFK (away from keyboard) will be kicked after 10 minutes and will not be allowed to rejoin for the following 15 minutes.

Additionally, land claims are forfeited after 60 days of in-game inactivity, and shops are purged after the same period.

7

Chat Language

This is an international server (Portuguese domain, hosted in Belgium and Spain). Please use English in the main chat and on Discord.

You may use other languages via direct messages: /msg <username> <message>.

Please report any incident of racism, sexism, or bullying — in any language — to staff.
