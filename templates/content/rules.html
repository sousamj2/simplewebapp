Mostly Jovial Crafters - Server Rules

1. Mutual Respect

Be friendly and respect other players. Harassment, racism, sexism, or any form of discrimination will not be tolerated.

2. No Griefing or Stealing

Do not destroy other players' builds or steal items from their chests, even if they are unprotected.

3. Use of Mods and Hacks

The use of modified clients, hacks, x-ray, or any unfair advantage is strictly prohibited. Allowed mods include Optifine/Sodium and minimaps (without entity radar).

4. Bug Exploitation (Exploits)

Do not abuse game or server bugs for your own benefit (item duplication, etc.).

5. PvP (Player vs Player)

Combat between players is only allowed if both parties agree.
