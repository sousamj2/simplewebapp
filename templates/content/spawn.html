The Book Shop at Spawn sells enchantment books at fixed prices. All books are stocked at their maximum enchantment level (e.g. Looting III, Efficiency V).

These books can normally be obtained from Librarian villagers. However, due to the server's trade rebalancing, each biome's Librarian specialises in different books — for example, a Plains Librarian offers different enchantments than a Desert or Swamp one. The shop provides all of them in one convenient location.

The last column contains Swift Sneak (levels III, II, and I — top to bottom). Swift Sneak can only be found in Ancient Cities and its strength increases with level.

Spawn stocks both Armour Trim Smithing Templates and Netherite Upgrade Templates at fixed prices.

  • Buying one template costs 1 Diamond Block (€10,000)
  • The seller receives a bonus of 2 diamonds (~€3,000) on top of the base price
  • Duplicating any template costs 7 diamonds (standard vanilla crafting)

Every player is entitled to 10 free general-purpose shops at Spawn, at no cost.

You can sell anything you like in your 10 free shops — there are no restrictions on item type or price. This includes enchantment books and smithing templates at your own prices, if you prefer not to use the dedicated sections at the fixed rates.

The Spawn Peninsula is the designated district where players can build and run their own in-game shops, attracting customers from across the server.

The Spawn Peninsula — community shop district (click to enlarge)

  1. Create a claim somewhere in the world to build your shop safely.
  2. Build your shop — including the chest — but do not create QuickShop listings yet.
  3. Request a plot on the Peninsula from ADMIN.
  4. After payment is deducted, ADMIN uses WorldEdit to move the shop to the Peninsula.
  5. Once moved, items, prices, and shop ownership are transferred to you.
  • A 10×10 block area costs €10,000 — This is the smallest shop size.
  • Any additional blocks cost €100 per block
  • The plot is always billed as a rectangle
💡 Note: Having your shop at Spawn is optional — you can operate shops anywhere on the map. However, being at Spawn will attract significantly more customers since all players pass through here.
